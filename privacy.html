What this page covers

This policy explains what 5 Apollo LLC collects when you use this website or send in a merchant statement for review.

What we collect

Only what you give us: your name, business name, email address, phone number, anything you write in the message field, and any merchant statement you send by email. Nothing is collected automatically beyond standard web server logs.

What we do with it

Your contact details are used to respond to you. A statement you send is used to calculate your effective rate and prepare a pricing comparison, and for nothing else. We do not sell, rent or share your information with third parties for marketing.

If you decide to open a merchant account, the information required to underwrite it is shared with PayBotX LLC and the sponsoring bank and processor, who handle it under their own privacy terms.

How long we keep it

Statements and analyses are retained for 3 months and then deleted, unless you become a client, in which case records are kept as long as required for the account and by professional standards.

Cookies and tracking

This site does not set advertising or analytics cookies.
